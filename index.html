Our Philosophy

Since our founding in 2005, Aymstrato Capital has been dedicated to excellence in investment management through a commitment to fundamental research, disciplined execution, and alignment of interests.

Discretion & Precision in Capital Allocation

Aymstrato Capital manages $12.4 billion in assets for a select group of institutional investors, family offices, and high-net-worth individuals. Our investment philosophy is built on the principle that true value is created through patience, discipline, and rigorous analysis.

We employ a concentrated portfolio approach, investing in businesses with durable competitive advantages, exceptional management teams, and attractive long-term growth prospects. Our team of seasoned investment professionals brings decades of collective experience across market cycles.

At Aymstrato Capital, we believe that alignment of interests is paramount. Our partners have significant personal capital invested alongside our clients, ensuring our incentives are fully aligned with long-term performance.
